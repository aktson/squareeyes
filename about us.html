What is SquareEyes

SquareEyes is video streaming website for independent filmmakers to stream videos on a pay-per-view basis. SquareEyess offers a Subscription Video-On-Demand Service, providing premium content directly to consumers.

SquareEyes emphasise on that films are independently produced and are crafted by people who are passionate about making films, for entusiasts who are passionate about watching high-quality films.
